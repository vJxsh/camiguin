About Katunggan Mangrove Park

Katunggan Mangrove Park is a thriving eco-tourism destination in Camiguin, located in Mahinog. It features a dense mangrove forest that protects the coastal ecosystem.

Visitors can walk along a wooden boardwalk through the mangroves while enjoying peaceful nature views.

The park is also important for environmental conservation and marine biodiversity.

Quick Facts

  • 🌿 Mangrove ecosystem
