About Tuasan Falls

Tuasan Falls is one of Camiguin’s most beautiful and accessible waterfalls, located in the town of Catarman.

Surrounded by lush forest and volcanic rock formations, the waterfall cascades into a cool and refreshing natural pool.

It is easily accessible by road, making it a popular destination for both locals and tourists.

Quick Facts

  • 📍 Catarman, Camiguin
  • 💦 Natural waterfall
  • 🚗 Roadside access + short walk
  • 🌿 Best: Morning / Early afternoon
  • 🎒 Wear non-slip footwear
  • 📸 Strong cascade & clear pool
