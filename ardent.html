About Ardent Hot Springs

Ardent Hot Springs is one of Camiguin’s most popular natural attractions, located at the foot of Mount Hibok-Hibok in Mambajao.

The springs are naturally heated by volcanic activity, providing warm and soothing waters ideal for relaxation.

Surrounded by lush forest, the cascading pools create a peaceful environment for visitors.
