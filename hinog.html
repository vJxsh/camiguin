About Mahinog

Mahinog is a coastal municipality in Camiguin known for its beautiful beaches and as the gateway to some of the island’s most famous attractions.

It is home to Mantigue Island, a popular destination for its white sand, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life perfect for snorkeling.

With its laid-back atmosphere and scenic coastal views, Mahinog offers a relaxing experience for visitors looking to enjoy nature and island life.
