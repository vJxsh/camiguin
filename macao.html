About Macao Cold Spring

Macao Cold Spring is a peaceful and refreshing natural attraction located in the town of Catarman, Camiguin.

Known for its cool, crystal-clear waters, the spring provides a relaxing escape from the island’s tropical heat.

Surrounded by lush greenery, it features a natural swimming area perfect for relaxation and unwinding in a calm environment.

Quick Facts

  • 📍 Catarman, Camiguin
  • 💧 Natural Cold Spring
  • 🏊 Swimming & Relaxation
  • 🕒 Best visit: Morning / Midday
  • 🎒 Bring snacks & extra clothes
  • 📸 Clear waters & peaceful scenery
