About Moro Watch Tower

The Moro Watch Tower in Camiguin is a historical structure built during the Spanish colonial period. It served as a lookout point against Moro pirate raids.

Located in Guinsiliban, it now stands as a cultural landmark and historical reminder of the island’s past defenses.

Visitors can enjoy scenic coastal views while learning about Camiguin’s rich history.
