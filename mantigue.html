About Mantigue Island

Mantigue Island is a small uninhabited island off the coast of Mahinog, Camiguin. It is known for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters.

The island is surrounded by a protected marine sanctuary, making it a great spot for snorkeling and diving.

It offers a peaceful tropical escape with views of Mount Hibok-Hibok in the distance.
