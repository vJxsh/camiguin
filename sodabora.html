About Soda Bora Water Spring

Soda Bora Water Spring is one of Camiguin's most unique natural attractions, known for its naturally carbonated spring water.

Located in Catarman, the spring has a light fizzy sensation similar to soda, giving it its unique name.

It is a refreshing swimming spot surrounded by lush greenery and a peaceful environment.

Quick Facts

  • 📍 Catarman, Camiguin
  • 💧 Natural soda spring
  • 🏊 Swimming activity
  • 🕒 Best: Morning / afternoon
  • 🎒 Bring extra clothes
  • 📸 Known for fizzy spring water
