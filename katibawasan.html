About Katibawasan Falls

Katibawasan Falls is one of Camiguin’s most iconic natural attractions, located in Mambajao. It cascades from a height of around 70 meters into a cool natural pool.

Surrounded by lush tropical forest, it offers a refreshing and scenic environment perfect for relaxation and photography.

It remains one of the most visited waterfalls in the province due to its accessibility and beauty.

Quick Facts

  • 💦 Natural waterfall
  • 🏊 Swimming allowed
