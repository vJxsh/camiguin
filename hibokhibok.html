About Mount Hibok-Hibok

Mount Hibok-Hibok is an active stratovolcano located in Camiguin near Mambajao.

It is one of the most iconic natural landmarks in the province and offers a challenging hiking experience.

The mountain features rich biodiversity, hot and cold springs, and panoramic summit views of Camiguin and the Bohol Sea.

Quick Facts

  • 🌋 Active stratovolcano
  • 🥾 Hiking & trekking

  • 📸 Summit & volcanic views
