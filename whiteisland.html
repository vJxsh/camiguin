About White Island

White Island is a stunning uninhabited sandbar located off the northern coast of Camiguin, Philippines.

It is famous for its powdery white sand, crystal-clear waters, and breathtaking views of Mount Hibok-Hibok.

With no trees or structures, the island offers a pure natural experience perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and photography.

Visitors typically reach the island via a short boat ride from Agoho.

Quick Facts

  • 📍 Camiguin, Philippines
  • 🏝️ Natural Sandbar
  • ⛵ 10–15 min boat ride
  • ☀️ Best: Sunrise / Sunset
  • 🎒 Bring sunscreen & water
  • 📸 360° ocean views
