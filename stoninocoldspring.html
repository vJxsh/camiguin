About Sto. Niño Cold Spring

Sto. Niño Cold Spring is a natural freshwater pool in Catarman, Camiguin, known for its crystal-clear and refreshing cold water.

Surrounded by lush greenery, it is a perfect destination for relaxation, swimming, and family outings.

Quick Facts

  • 📍 Catarman, Camiguin
  • 💧 Natural cold spring
  • 🌡️ ~20°C water
  • 🕒 Best: Morning / Midday
  • 🎒 Bring extra clothes
  • 📸 Clear blue water
